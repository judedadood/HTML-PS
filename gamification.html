Gamification

Monopoly

Monopoly is a classic board game that teaches essential financial principles through fun and competitive gameplay. Players navigate the board, buying and selling properties, building houses and hotels, and managing their money to avoid bankruptcy. The game teaches important financial concepts.

Start Playing

Budgeting

A monthly budgeting game simulates real-life financial planning and management, teaching youth how to create and adhere to a budget. In this game, players receive a virtual monthly income and must allocate it to various expenses such as rent, groceries, entertainment, and savings.

Start Playing

Cash flow

The calculating monthly cash flow game is designed to help youth understand the importance of cash flow management. Players are tasked with managing a simulated personal or business budget, tracking all income and expenses to maintain a positive cash flow.
